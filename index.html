Twinkling Personalities

Astrology has become increasing popular in American internet culture, with more and more essays coming out explaining your sign. Learn about which words and sentiments are most frequently apparent in descriptions of the twelve zodiac signs based on a sample of American web publications. Are you an emotional Cancer? Or a passionate Aries? See which signs scored highest for each sentiment.

Word Association

Can you describe yourself in three words? Click through to see which words came up most frequently when describing each zodiac sign. Decide if you agree with what the astrologists have to say about you. Do you notice any similarities between the different elements? Or perhaps you are just wondering how Scorpio isn't at least 10% fire?
